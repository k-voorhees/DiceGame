Player 1 starts the game.
During each turn, the current player can choose to roll the dice, or hold.
Your score goes up by the number you roll.
However, if a player rolls a 1, then all built up score during that turn is lost and it becomes the next player's turn.
If you select hold, all of your current score for that round is saved and added to your total score, and you pass your turn to the next player.

First player to 100 points wins.
